Overview

Purpose

My plan is to create my Portfolio Website. This will give me the opportunity to share my biography and show the work that I am doing as a web developer. The portfolio will allow me to receive new job offers.

Audience

I want to show my portfolio website to employers from all over the world who are looking for new web developers. They are looking for professionalism, seriousness and experience, and that is what I want to show them.
